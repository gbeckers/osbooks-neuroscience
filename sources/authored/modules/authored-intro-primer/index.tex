% title: Preface

This primer on background topics for the course UCSCICOG11,
Cognitive Neuroscience I, mostly consists of modules from various
OpenStax books (see Provenance \& Attribution section at the end of
this document). Where no suitable existing modules could be found, some
subsections were written by the course teachers (currently the
\textit{Ions} subsection).

The materials in this primer are intended for students who have
little background in biology, or those who need a refresher. The
selected background topics are prerequisite knowledge for the course,
but they are not themselves examinable course material. We recommend
that students with little background in biology take UCSCIBIO01,
\textit{That's Life: Biology Today}, first; however, if they do not,
this primer will help them learn the topics that we have noticed such
students tend to struggle with.

The topics are arranged in the order in which they are first introduced
in the course.
