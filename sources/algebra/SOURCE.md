## Notes

Section 6.3 (Logarithmic Functions) of *College Algebra 2e*, imported to give the 
readers who don't know how logarithms work the chance to fix this. The original 
OpenStax module is adapted. The excercises at the start are removed. OpenStax does 
also not render them in their online version. Module directories 
are prefixed `alg-adapt` so their ids stay globally unique; the modules are otherwise 
unmodified from upstream.
