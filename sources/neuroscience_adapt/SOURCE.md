## Notes
neuroscience_adapt contains modules that originate from the neuroscience book, but that have been adapted, e.g. 
shortened, errors fixed, or expanded. We keep them separate from the original modules, which we want to track in case 
openstax introduces changes. Module directories are prefixed `neuro-adap-` so their ids
stay globally unique; the modules are otherwise unmodified from upstream.
